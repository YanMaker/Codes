How to Make Guacamole

Ingredients:

  • 2 avocados
  • 1 lime
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Cilantro
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Peel the avocados and plant the pit to have your own avocado plant. Then, mash the avocados in a bowl.
  2. Dice the tomatoes and mix with the avocados.
  3. Chop the cilantro and mix in the bowl.
  4. Split the lime in two and drizzle the mix in lime juice.
  5. Add black pepper and salt and mix thoroughly.
  6. Enjoy!
